<Info>Coinflow off-ramping is currently only available for user bank accounts in the US.</Info>

## What is Coinflow?

Coinflow develops software bridging conventional finance and blockchain tech, enabling users to purchase digital assets and services using standard payment methods. With top-tier US bank collaborations, Coinflow provides instant 24/7 crypto off-ramping to user bank accounts.

## How to off-ramp assets to a bank account

### To off-ramp assets to a business's bank account:

1. Navigate to the "Treasury" tab, click the "Send" button, and select the "Off-ramp" option.

<img src="https://docs.squads.so/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F254049203-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FdUrslJzNhqr2JDZzXUUz%252Fuploads%252FV89DeKfVBfotIimtPlMR%252FOff-ramp%25201.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3Dc1dcfcc8-1b8a-4a59-bd86-78f270617c6b&width=768&dpr=2&quality=100&sign=dd65e716&sv=1" />

2. Select "Business" in the pop-up.

<img src="https://docs.squads.so/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F254049203-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FdUrslJzNhqr2JDZzXUUz%252Fuploads%252FwLftkUc1WQkI67tS4KoI%252FOff-ramp%25202.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D40ae9a55-5d22-49bd-ab51-3314c326af99&width=768&dpr=2&quality=100&sign=51cd6a7d&sv=1" />

3. Fill in the following info about your business:

   - Legal business name
   - US Tax ID number
   - Company website
   - Business address
   - Your name and e-mail

<img src="https://docs.squads.so/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F254049203-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FdUrslJzNhqr2JDZzXUUz%252Fuploads%252FwUrLVbcUzBa4Pf9EapSn%252Foff-ramp%2520business.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3Dfdd7f1ea-7aae-4def-950d-59aeddf10ce4&width=768&dpr=2&quality=100&sign=8f897063&sv=1" />

4. Click submit. Coinflow will transfer funds to your bank account after processing your request.

### To off-ramp assets to your individual bank account:

1. Navigate to the "Treasury" tab, click the "Send" button, and select the "Off-ramp" option.

<img src="https://docs.squads.so/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F254049203-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FdUrslJzNhqr2JDZzXUUz%252Fuploads%252F1BNxbI37YAtnDOodikYX%252FOff-ramp%25201.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D34ecf300-8f98-4815-9e46-a580a8a43c02&width=768&dpr=2&quality=100&sign=d002dffb&sv=1" />

2. Select "Person" in the pop-up.

<img src="https://docs.squads.so/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F254049203-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FdUrslJzNhqr2JDZzXUUz%252Fuploads%252FwLftkUc1WQkI67tS4KoI%252FOff-ramp%25202.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D40ae9a55-5d22-49bd-ab51-3314c326af99&width=768&dpr=2&quality=100&sign=51cd6a7d&sv=1" />

3. Fill in the personal information:

   - Name and Last name
   - Address
   - Birthday
   - E-mail
   - Last 4 of SSN number

<img src="https://docs.squads.so/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F254049203-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FdUrslJzNhqr2JDZzXUUz%252Fuploads%252FW7s9tTj8zJxWXLJ9Bp4E%252FOff-ramp%2520personal.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3Da94a087a-f2d7-47b7-b0e1-478a0535fd7f&width=768&dpr=2&quality=100&sign=a6f6417f&sv=1" />

4.  Click "Complete verification". Coinflow will transfer funds to your bank account after processing your request.
